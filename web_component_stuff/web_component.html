Limgrave is the starting area of the game, so welcome in! Here you will find a beautiful lake (beware the dragon...), wonderful hills, and beautiful beaches.

If you love water, Liurnia is your place! Theres a lake and.... not much else. There's thin strips of land on the outskirts of Liurnia, but the main focus here is the gorgeous Academy of Raya Lucaria built in the center of the lake.

If you want to come here... no you don't. Caelid is hell on The Lands Between. Make your trip short, sweet, and to the point, and you should do just fine (as long as you are overleveled, at least)!
